ds_uid : Unique-ID of driveshed/cluster (n=92).
population : Total population in driveshed/cluster based on https://usafactsstatic.blob.core.windows.net/public/data/covid-19/covid_county_population_usafacts.csv
report : Coming total count of confirmed cases over the next three weeks for each cluster of counties.
total : Coming total count of confirmed cases per 100,000 residents over the next three weeks for each cluster of counties.
report_per_100K : Coming total count of infected cases over the next three weeks for each cluster of counties.
total_per_100K : Coming total count of infected cases per 100,000 residents over the next three weeks for each cluster of counties.
weightedR : Population weighted average of driveshed/cluster. Formula (based on 3 counties) = (R1*pop1+R2*pop2+R3*pop3)/(pop1+pop2+pop3)
